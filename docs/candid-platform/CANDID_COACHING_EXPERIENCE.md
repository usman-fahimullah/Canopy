## The Mentee/Coach Relationship

### Core Principles

1. **You have a coach** (not "find a coach")
   - Saathe Studio is your dedicated coaching partner
   - Your coach knows your story, goals, and progress
   - The relationship persists across sessions

2. **Progress happens between sessions**
   - Async messaging for quick questions
   - Structured homework/assignments
   - Self-guided learning resources
   - Coach reviews your work async

3. **Clear milestones, not just sessions**
   - Career clarity â†’ Job-ready â†’ Interview-ready â†’ Offer
   - Both mentee and coach track progress
   - Celebrate wins together

## Questions to Resolve

1. **Session cadence:** How often do mentees meet with coach? Weekly? Bi-weekly?
2. **Program duration:** Is there a defined program length? Or ongoing until job landed?
3. **Pricing model:** Subscription? Package? Per-session?
4. **Coach assignment:** Is it always Radhika, or a team member?
5. **Content ownership:** Does Saathe provide courses, or does Candid create them?
6. **Community moderation:** Who moderates peer channels?
7. **Mentor incentives:** How do we attract and retain volunteer mentors?
8. **Group session pricing:** Included in coaching, or separate?
